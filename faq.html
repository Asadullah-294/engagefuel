Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses, enrollment process, and platform features

To enroll in a course, simply click the "Enroll Now" button on any course page. You'll be prompted to sign up or log in if you haven't already. After completing the payment process (for paid courses), you'll gain immediate access to the course materials.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) as well as PayPal. For enterprise or group purchases, we also accept bank transfers - please contact our sales team for arrangements.

Most of our courses require only a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge). Some programming courses may require free software like Visual Studio Code or Python, which will be clearly indicated in the course requirements. All necessary tools are either free or have free alternatives available.

Yes! You can enroll in multiple courses simultaneously and progress through them at your own pace. Our platform allows you to easily switch between courses, and you'll have lifetime access to any course you purchase, so you can always come back to complete it later.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all courses. If you're not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, simply contact our support team within 30 days of enrollment for a full refund, no questions asked.

You get lifetime access to all courses you purchase. This includes all future updates to the course content at no additional cost. Free courses remain accessible as long as they're offered on our platform.

Our technical support team is available 24/7 via email at support@engagefuel.store. For urgent issues, you can also request a callback through our contact form. Most technical questions receive a response within 4 hours.

Yes! All paid courses include a verifiable certificate of completion that you can download and share on LinkedIn or include in your resume. To earn a certificate, you'll need to complete all course modules and pass the final assessment with a score of 80% or higher.

We offer significant discounts for group enrollments (5+ students). Discounts start at 20% for groups of 5 and can go up to 50% for enterprise purchases. Contact our team at groups@engagefuel.store for a customized quote based on your needs.

Our platform works on any modern device with an internet connection. Minimum requirements include:

  • Windows 10/macOS 10.15 or later, or recent Linux distribution
  • Chrome 90+, Firefox 85+, Safari 14+, or Edge 90+
  • 4GB RAM (8GB recommended for programming courses)
  • 10MBps internet connection (for video streaming)
